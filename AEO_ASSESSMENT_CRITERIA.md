Content Formatting & Structure: 30%
This category receives a joint-top weighting because if your content is not structured for easy machine extraction, it will be ignored by AI entirely—even if your website ranks #1 on Google. In AEO, you are optimizing a specific passage or "chunk," not just a full page.
The Inverted Pyramid / Answer Capsules (15%): This is the single highest-leverage change you can make. AI models scan for a concise 40–60 word declarative answer placed immediately beneath a heading
Machine-Readable Formatting (10%): Generative engines favor content broken into lists, bullet points, and HTML tables. In fact, Google's AI Overviews feature lists in 78% of cases
Question-Driven Headings (5%): Using H2 and H3 tags that directly mirror natural language queries (e.g., "What are the benefits of retinol?") helps algorithms instantly match user intent to your content

E-E-A-T & Authority Signals: 30%
Tied for the highest weighting, E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness) is described as a "non-negotiable prerequisite" for AEO. Because AI engines are risk-averse and programmed to prevent "hallucinations" (false information), they use E-E-A-T as a technical trust filter.
Information Gain & Original Experience (15%): AI models prioritize content containing original research, primary data, and firsthand experience because it provides value the AI cannot generate from its own training data
Brand Authority & External Citations (10%): Answer engines evaluate your entire digital footprint. Being consistently cited or mentioned by high-authority external sources (like Forbes, Reddit, or industry journals) acts as a massive trust signal that tells the AI you are a definitive source
Explicit Authorship (5%): Clearly attributing content to real people with verifiable identities, credentials, and bios is a critical trust signal for both Google and LLMs

Technical AEO & Accessibility: 20%
Technical health serves as the foundational "entry ticket" for AI visibility. If an AI crawler cannot access or parse your content, your optimization efforts are useless.
Schema Markup (12%): Applying JSON-LD structured data is paramount. Research shows that proper schema implementation increases AI citation probability by 30% to 36%. FAQPage schema is uniquely powerful, as it pre-formats question-and-answer pairs for direct extraction, driving up to 28% higher citation rates
Raw HTML & DOM Simplicity (5%): Many AI bots (unlike Googlebot) have limited time budgets and struggle to render complex JavaScript. Critical text must be accessible in raw HTML; if it is hidden behind dynamic tabs or scripts, it is effectively invisible to AI
Core Web Vitals (3%): Fast load speeds (LCP under 2.5 seconds) and site stability are baseline requirements, as AI crawlers may abandon slow-loading pages

Entity & Semantic Alignment: 15%
Search has transitioned from "pattern matching" keywords to understanding the relationships between concepts. AI models "think" in entities (people, places, things, concepts) and map them to Knowledge Graphs.
Entity Salience & Consistency (10%): You must clearly establish what your content is about by using exact, recognized terminology rather than vague descriptors. Emphasizing an entity in your H1/H2 tags and early paragraphs yields a high "salience score," signaling strong topical focus to the AI.
Topic Clustering (5%): Grouping your content into a "pillar page" connected to specific subtopics builds a semantic web. This demonstrates "Topical Authority," proving to the AI that your site possesses deep, comprehensive knowledge on a subject.

Tracking & Refinement: 5%
While measurement does not directly cause your content to rank, it is a critical operational requirement. Because the AI landscape is shifting rapidly, traditional SEO metrics (like click-through rates) are no longer sufficient to measure success.
AI Share of Voice Tracking (5%): Your focus must shift from tracking SERP positions to monitoring "AI visibility"—specifically, how often your brand is cited or mentioned in generative responses across platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Regularly monitoring these citations enables you to test, adapt, and refine your content structure.
